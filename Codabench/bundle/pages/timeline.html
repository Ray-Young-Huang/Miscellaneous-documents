Time Period and Key Deliverables

The following schedule is formulated in accordance with Greenwich Mean Time (UTC+0)

  • Dec 1, 2025 - Dec 6, 2025; Challenge registration opens.
  • Dec 7, 2025 - Jan 1, 2026; Dataset and initial code release, providing a small amount of validation data for submitting tests.
  • Jan 2, 2026 - Jan 15, 2026; The validating phase begins.
  • Jan 16, 2026 - Jan 22, 2026; The test phase begins.
  • Jan 23, 2026 - Mar 1, 2026; The challenge organizer ranks the submitted algorithms according to the prescribed evaluation method.
  • Mar 2, 2026 - Mar 15, 2026; The participating teams who have completed the submission will be required to submit a report on the algorithm implementation.
  • Apr 8, 2026 - Apr 11, 2026; ISBI conference begins, announcing the list of winners.
